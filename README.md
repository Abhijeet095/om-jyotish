# ओम ज्योतिष

This is the website I built for my father's business, Om Jyotish.

My father, Jagdish Vaishnav, has been performing religious rituals and priesthood services for more than 20 years — things like weddings, munj (thread ceremony), naming ceremonies, housewarming, and other poojas and yagnas. He mainly serves families in Alandi, Bhosari, and the Pimpri-Chinchwad (PCMC) area.

I wanted to give his work a proper online presence, so people looking for a purohit in the area can easily find him, see what services he offers, and get in touch.
